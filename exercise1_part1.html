Messi

Highlight Compilation

Simply inhuman

His Strengths

  1. Dribbling
  2. Speed
  3. Passing
  4. Shooting

Specialties

  • He always finds a way to go through the defender, whereas other ordinary players would simply try to go around.
  • This makes him extremely hard to defend as well as unpredictable
  • Most of it is instinct and can only be learned with extreme dedication and willpower from a young age





For me, he is a player you can look up to, as well as copy the moves he is making.

Ronaldinho, Neymar or Ben Arfa are good as well, but they are not playing as effective as him.
